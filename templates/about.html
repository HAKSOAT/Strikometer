ASUU

The Academic Staff Union of Universities (ASUU) is a Nigerian union of university academic staff, founded in 1978.

Reasons For Current Strike Action

The ASUU commenced on an indefinite strike on the 5th of November 2018 due to issues of poor funding.
The National President of ASUU, Professor Abiodun Ogunyemi, directed all members of the union across various universities to withdraw their services, stressing that the strike would be total and indefinite.
